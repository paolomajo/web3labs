Our Vision

We develop and market software applications to facilitate the use of the innovative decentralized internet. After Web 1.0 and Web 2.0, we are evolving towards Web 3.0. Web3 Labs assists companies in this metamorphosis, sharing knowledge that will increasingly be needed in the business world. Never before has progress been able to offer a social boost, a redistribution of resources, a major improvement in society itself. The idea to better serve the web community was born in order to provide users a true open and democratic use of the web. Not only for big companies that take advantage of their dominance, but finally a space for small companies with a real competition between innovators. Decentralization is the key for a better world through a better new web: the Web 3.0 based on blockchain technology.

What

Our specific activity as an innovative start-up consists in the development and marketing of a web 3.0 browser platform based on blockchain technology and the relatively decentralized applications (Dapps). The core of our activity is cutting edge Software Research & Development and Marketing of a browser with various innovative browsing solutions, including among others browsing engines and related blockchain technologies, integration to various Blockchain Platforms and Dapps, development of various software Apps, API’s, Users and Developers manuals and SDK tools, software Integrations, Web3 Dapps, Cryptocurrencies Wallets and Marketplace platforms. High technological value is given by the use of register technology distributed (DLT, Distributed Ledger Technology) and by the interoperability protocols between machines (IoT, Internet of Things).

BLOCKCHAIN TECHNOLOGY - SHARING ECONOMY

The power of this new technology lies in the fact that it allows people and companies to store and securely transfer information and currency instantly. The term blockchain also refers to how the data is stored in ”blocks” of information and then linked together in a permanent “chain.” When a new block is added to the chain, all the information are copied, and it makes the previous blocks even harder to modify, which helps each block become more and more secure over time. This means technology becomes the medium among people, the certificate of reliability, and we can progressively eliminate the need for many many obsolete institutions.
